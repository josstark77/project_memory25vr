Instructions

You will be shown a sequence of flashes on the 3√ó3 grid. The order matters. After the sequence finishes, reproduce the sequence by tapping the same tiles in the same order.

You have 3 chances per level. If incorrect, the next attempt will display a new sequence for that same level. Complete the sequence correctly to proceed to the next level.
