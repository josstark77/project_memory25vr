Digit Span Test

How to Play

You will see digits one by one on the screen.

Your task is to remember the digits and type them in the same order.

Each level increases the length of digits.

If you fail once, you will get a second chance.
In the second chance, you must correctly complete two sequences to pass.
If you fail, the game is over.

Example:

Digits appear: 3 → 8 → 1
You enter: 381 ✅

Level: 1

Chance: 1



Game Over ❌
